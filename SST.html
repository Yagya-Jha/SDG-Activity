How are they Celebrated? Eco-friendly festivals are celebrated in such a way so as to bring minimum or no harm to the environment. It is through the initiative of each one of us that we can bring about eco-friendly festivals for years to come. Non-Eco-friendly festivals are celebrated in such a way that there is harm caused to the environment. It is celebrated for the fun of Humans and fullfilling our needs. We don't care about what will be the effect of all this on Nature and most importantly Mother Earth
